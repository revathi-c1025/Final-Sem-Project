## 🔧 Quick Customization Tips

### To Change Colors:
1. Select the text box
2. Go to Home → Font Color
3. Choose your preferred color

### To Change Fonts:
1. Select the text
2. Go to Home → Font
3. Choose your preferred font

### To Insert Images:
1. Click the placeholder box
2. Go to Insert → Pictures
3. Select your screenshot/diagram
4. Resize and position as needed

### To Delete Placeholders:
1. Click the placeholder text box
2. Press Delete
3. Insert your actual image
